What's needed to connect?

No additional mods or addon's are needed at this moment. Everything will be updated by Technic Launcher unless you prefer to use your own client-sided mods.

Having problems connecting or unsure where to get the correct launcher needed? Visit: http://www.technicpack.net/download and download the correct launcher needed for your operating system.

Once the download is completed and you went through the install process, make sure you change in Options the Java Ram Settings from default 1GB to at least 4GB - 5GB RAM. You will have constant crashing, with 1GB default ram, when loading the game.
